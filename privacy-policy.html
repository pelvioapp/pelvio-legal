Pelvio — Privacy Policy

Pelvic Floor Trainer App

The short version: Pelvio stores all your personal health data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. No account required. No servers.

1. Who We Are

Pelvio ("we," "us," or "our") is a pelvic floor trainer app developed for iOS and Android. Our app helps users perform guided Kegel exercises, track their progress, and learn about pelvic health.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: pelvio.app@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect — And What We Don't

Pelvio is designed to work completely offline with no account or sign-up required. Here is exactly what happens with your data:

  • All data stays on your device. Your workout history, streak, points, badges, goals, gender selection, and preferences are stored using your device's local storage only.
  • We do not collect personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying information.
  • We do not have servers or a database. There is no backend — nothing you do in the app is sent to us or any third party.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs. There are no third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries integrated into Pelvio.
  • We do not sell data. We have never sold and will never sell any user data to any third party.

3. Health Information

Pelvio may ask for general information such as biological sex (male/female) and your pelvic health goal (e.g. bladder control, postpartum recovery) to personalize your workout plan.

This information is stored exclusively on your local device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can delete this information at any time by using the "Reset all progress" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

4. Local Notifications

If you choose to enable workout reminders, Pelvio may request permission to send you local notifications. These notifications are:

  • Generated and scheduled entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to any external server
  • Fully under your control — you can disable them at any time in your device's notification settings or within the app

5. Children's Privacy

Pelvio is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child under 18 is using the app, please contact us at pelvio.app@gmail.com and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Third-Party Services

Pelvio does not currently integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. By downloading the app, you are subject to the respective store's privacy policy:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's own security features (passcode, biometric lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a screen lock.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app in the Progress and Settings screens.
  • Delete: Go to Settings → Reset all progress to permanently delete all locally stored data. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all data from your device.
  • Export: You can export a summary of your workout history using the Export button in the Progress screen.

If you are located in the European Union, you may have additional rights under GDPR. Since we do not collect or process any personal data on our servers, GDPR obligations related to data controller/processor relationships do not apply to our operations. All data processing happens locally on your device.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold. Pelvio does not collect, sell, or share any personal information. No personal information is transmitted off your device. Therefore, there is no personal information subject to CCPA rights requests. You may contact us at pelvio.app@gmail.com with any questions.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

For significant changes, we will update the version number in the app's Settings screen so you are aware a change has been made.
